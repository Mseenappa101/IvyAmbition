The 150-Character Framework

✓ Strong descriptions include:
  • A strong past-tense action verb as the first word
  • Numbers: members, participants, dollars raised, events organized
  • Your specific role and what you did, not just the club's purpose
  • Outcome or achievement if space allows
✗ Weak descriptions include:
  • Passive voice ("was responsible for", "helped with")
  • Generic club descriptions that any member could write
  • Vague language with no numbers or specifics
  • Restating the activity name or type in the description
