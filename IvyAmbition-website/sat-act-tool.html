When to Submit

  • Your score is at or above the school's 50th percentile
  • You're applying to a test-required school
  • Your score is in the middle 50% and your GPA is strong
  • You need test scores for scholarship consideration

When to Go Test-Optional

  • Your score is below the 25th percentile at that school
  • Your GPA and ECs are significantly stronger than your score
  • The school is genuinely test-optional (not just test-flexible)
  • You have a compelling story that a score might undercut
